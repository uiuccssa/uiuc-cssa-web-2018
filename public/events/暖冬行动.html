暖冬行动

暖冬行动, 是一场由CSSA学生事务部微光计划小组举办的捐赠活动，我们的目标是在农历新年是在寒冬里稳当地无家可归的街友们 送上一点温暖。很快就要过年了，希望在我们过年的时候，也希望能让更多人在过年的时候，得到一些来自这个世界的温暖。

时间：2018年1月26日至2018年2月16日，每周五中午12：30到1：30

地点：Union门口

“我们是微光计划，一个由一群热心而有理想的UIUC留学生建立的非盈利性公益项目。”

Warm Chinese New Year

“Warm Chinese New Year” Action is a clothing and food drive activity started by Glimmer Project, a project belong to UIUC CSSA Department of Student Affairs. Our goal is to donate some clothing as well as can food to the local Homeless group. It is going to be Chinese New Year, and we hope to get more people enjoy the time, and enjoy the little but true kindness from the world.

Time: Jan. 26 to Feb. 16, every Friday from 12:30 to 1:30

Location: Front of Illni Union

"We are Glimmer Project, we would like to offer the world some Glimmer of kindness."
